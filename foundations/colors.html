The color palette is a cornerstone of Quizlet, giving the product an immediate sense of being familiar and warm. The color palette leverages Quizlet’s brand heritage, and builds on its sense of energy, intelligence and confidence. It adds warmth and a human touch.

Quizlet is committed to complying with AA standard contrast ratios for all text that is not “incidental”. The color combinations in this guide don’t comply with the AA standard just yet. Make sure you don’t rely on color alone to convey state or meaning.

Primary color palette

Our primary palette consists of neutral grays, white, blue, and navy. These colors are represented in the UI chrome while reserving the secondary palette when pops of energy and color are necessary. Download the sketch palette here.
