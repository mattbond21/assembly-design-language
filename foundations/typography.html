Typography is critical to our users success. The content that users input into Quizlet should be the focal point of their experience while the remaining UI serves as a series of containers to enhance their studying. We’ve carefully choosen a hierarchy and type scale that gives clarity and also has enough density to feel like a study tool, not a website.

Typefaces

We utilize Hurme Geometric Sans 2 on all platforms to ensure a Quizlet feel when users move between platforms.

font-family: hurme_no2-webfont, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", "Roboto", "Noto Sans", "Ubuntu", "Droid Sans", "Helvetica Neue", sans-serif;

Headings

Using a consist and logical hierarchy ensures that elements in the UI are clear and easily recognizable when scanning the page. Text sizes, styles and layouts were selected to balance content and UI and to foster familiarity.
